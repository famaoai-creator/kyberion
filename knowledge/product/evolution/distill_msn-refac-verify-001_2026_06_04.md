# Standardized Refactor-Regression Verification and Multi-Target Ticket Dispatch

## Summary
Successfully verified the completion of a refactor-regression mission, ensuring all coordination tickets were registered across Jira, GitHub, and internal systems before advancing to the distillation phase.

## Key Learnings
- Explicit verification of distillation readiness ensures that knowledge extraction is triggered only after all mission deliverables are accounted for.
- Multi-platform ticket synchronization (Jira, GitHub, Workitem) creates a robust audit trail for distributed development teams.

## Patterns Discovered
- Centralized Ticket Dispatch Pattern: Using a unified manifest to coordinate task state across heterogeneous tracking platforms during a mission lifecycle.
