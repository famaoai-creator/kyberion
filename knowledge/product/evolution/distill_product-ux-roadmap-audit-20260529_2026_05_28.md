# Product UX Evaluation & Roadmap Alignment Audit

## Summary
Evaluated end-user usability and updated the product roadmap by synthesizing data from baseline checks, vitals, and 'first-win' smoke tests.

## Key Learnings
- Audit accuracy is significantly improved by synthesizing data across multiple infrastructure layers, including baseline, vitals, and surface status reports.
- Validating 'first-win' smoke evidence against the UX Contract provides a concrete feedback loop for confirming that technical milestones deliver intended user value.

## Patterns Discovered
- Pattern: Multi-layered health report synthesis (baseline, vital, setup) as a prerequisite for strategic roadmap validation.

## Reusable Artifacts
- Product UX evaluation document
- Roadmap addendum incorporating cross-layer report synthesis
