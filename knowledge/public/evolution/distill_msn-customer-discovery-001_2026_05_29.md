# Structured Customer Discovery via Persona-Based Critique and Validation Gates

## Summary
Executed a structured discovery mission for Deal Tracker using transcript extraction, multi-persona cross-critique, and gate-based validation to produce a high-fidelity product roadmap.

## Key Learnings
- Multi-persona fanout (3-persona) effectively identifies diverse solution paths and potential risks early in discovery.
- Explicit gate validation (e.g., REQUIREMENTS_COMPLETENESS) forces identification of critical data gaps like budget constraints.

## Patterns Discovered
- Transcript-to-Roadmap Pipeline: Chaining extraction, fanout critique, and roadmap synthesis for consistent discovery output.
- Strategic Verification Gates: Using mission history to track pending stakeholder sign-offs and open questions.

## Failures & Recoveries
- Requirement gap identified (budget constraints) → Logged as open dependency in roadmap rather than stalling the mission phase.
