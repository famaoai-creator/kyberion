## How releases are tagged

We use Conventional Commits (`feat:`, `fix:`, `chore:`, `docs:`, `refactor:`, `test:`, `breaking:`).

- `feat:` and `feat(scope):` → minor bump (or major if `BREAKING CHANGE:` footer)
- `fix:` → patch bump
- `breaking:` or `BREAKING CHANGE:` footer → major bump
- everything else → no bump (rolled into the next minor/patch)

When a release is tagged, `scripts/generate_changelog.ts` populates a new section above with the commits since the previous tag.
